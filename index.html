Biography of Virat Kohli

Biography

Virat Kohli (born in 5 November 1988. He is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is a right-handed batsman and an occasional medium-fast bowler. He currently represents Royal Challengers Bengaluru in the IPL and Delhi in domestic cricket. Kohli is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time.)

Career Achievements

Virat Kohli is known for his exceptional batting prowess and has broken numerous records in international cricket.
